What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a numerical measure of a person's weight in relation to their height. It is widely used as an indicator of whether a person has a healthy body weight.

Why is BMI Important?

BMI is a useful tool for assessing your health. It helps identify if you are underweight, overweight, or within a healthy weight range. Maintaining a healthy BMI can reduce the risk of various health issues.

Calculating BMI

Our BMI calculator uses your weight and height to calculate your BMI. It provides information on your BMI category and health status.
